About the client

Daikin Airconditioning Philippines

DAIKIN is a long-standing enterprise client of edamame, headquartered in Pasay, Metro Manila. Daikin is one of the world's largest air conditioning manufacturers, with global headquarters in Osaka, Japan.

Out of respect for DAIKIN's internal operations, we don't publish the specific workflows, apps, or automations deployed for them. What we can share is the relationship itself: 2 continuous years of Kintone deployment, covering 450+ users, with edamame as the sole implementation and support partner throughout.

Why DAIKIN chose edamame

Every enterprise Kintone engagement in the Philippines has four partner choices: go direct to Cybozu Japan (no local support, JP-only working hours), self-implement (no Kintone expertise, slow), engage a generic IT services firm (no Kintone specialization), or engage the official Philippine partner. DAIKIN chose the last.

The reasons long-running clients give us publicly: local Philippine hours support, bilingual English/Japanese delivery, deep Kintone platform knowledge, and the ability to handle the full stack from app design through JavaScript customization through AI integration without subcontracting.

Enterprise software engagements that last 2 years across hundreds of users are rare. They happen when four things are true: the platform fits, the partner executes, the business outcomes compound, and the switching cost of changing is perpetually lower than the value of staying. DAIKIN has been a Kintone + edamame customer through all four.
