About the client

Maximum Solutions Corporation

Founded in March 2001 as a manpower services provider, Maximum Solutions Corporation (MSC) has evolved into a one-stop IT solutions and outsourcing firm. The company is 100% Filipino-owned and serves industries ranging from architecture and construction to manufacturing and education. MSC is a recognized distributor of Autodesk and Samsung products in the Philippines, and has delivered technology integrations for Philippine enterprises including Megawide Construction Corporation, ESCA Incorporated, and Life Academy International School.

Why MS Corp chose edamame
