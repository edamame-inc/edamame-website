About the client

Guhring Philippines Inc.

Guhring Philippines Inc. (GPI) is the Philippine subsidiary of Gühring KG, the German manufacturer of rotary precision cutting tools founded in 1898. Gühring KG employs approximately 8,000 people across 70+ production locations in 48 countries. GPI established a representative office in the Philippines in 2009, was formed as a direct subsidiary in 2014, and today serves Philippine manufacturers across automotive, aerospace, machinery manufacturing, general engineering, and construction sectors. GPI's product range includes drills, end mills, taps, reamers, countersinks, and deburring tools — imported directly from Germany and stocked locally in Alabang. The company also offers tool management services including the Guhring TM-MINIBOX digital tool management system.
