ROI: When Does Kintone Pay for Itself?

Most Philippine businesses see ROI within 2-3 months. The math is simple: if Kintone saves each user 30 minutes per day on manual processes (Excel entry, email chasing, paper approvals), that's 10 hours/month. At an average Philippine salary, those saved hours are worth more than the subscription cost.

Enterprise clients like ORIX Metro and Daikin Philippines report that Kintone replaced 3-5 separate software subscriptions while adding capabilities they didn't have before.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is Kintone per user in the Philippines?
