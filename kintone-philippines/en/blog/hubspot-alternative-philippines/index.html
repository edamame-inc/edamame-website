Best HubSpot Alternatives Philippines 2026: 5 Platforms That Cost Less and Do More

Looking for a HubSpot alternative in the Philippines? Compare Kintone, Zoho, Pipedrive, Freshsales, and Bitrix24 — with real pricing in pesos and honest feature comparison.

📅 2026-04-13 ⏱ 11 min read ✍️ Tom Arai, CEO — Edamame Inc.

Why Philippine Businesses Are Leaving HubSpot

HubSpot is a powerful platform. But for many Philippine businesses, the math simply doesn't work. Once you move past the free tier, costs escalate quickly — Professional plans start at ₱45,000+/month, mandatory onboarding fees can hit ₱170,000, and you're paying in USD with foreign transaction fees on top.

The result? Many Philippine SMEs sign up for the free plan, outgrow it, then face a painful choice: pay enterprise prices or migrate to something else. Here are five alternatives worth considering.

1. Kintone — Best for CRM + Operations (from ₱1,000/user/month)

Kintone isn't a traditional CRM — it's a no-code platform where you build exactly the CRM your business needs. Track leads, manage deals, automate approvals, and connect it to your entire operation. Used by ORIX Metro, Daikin Philippines, and 50+ other enterprises.

Why switch from HubSpot: You get CRM + project management + workflow automation + reporting in one platform. No per-contact pricing. No mandatory onboarding fees. Filipino, English, and Japanese support from Edamame Inc., the official Kintone partner in the Philippines.

Best for: Companies that need CRM plus operational workflows — not just marketing automation.

2. Zoho CRM — Best Budget All-Rounder (from ~₱800/user/month)

Zoho offers a massive suite of 55+ apps at prices that undercut HubSpot significantly. The Standard CRM plan gives you lead management, scoring, workflows, and reporting at roughly ₱800/user/month.

The catch: Zoho's interface can feel dated, and the sheer number of apps creates decision fatigue. Integration between Zoho apps isn't always seamless. But for pure CRM features at a low price, it's hard to beat.

Best for: Cost-conscious teams that primarily need contact management and email tracking.

3. Pipedrive — Best for Sales-Only Teams (~₱1,400/user/month)

Pipedrive is laser-focused on sales pipeline management. The visual pipeline interface is excellent, and the mobile app works well for Philippine field sales teams. No marketing automation, no service desk — just clean deal tracking.

Best for: Pure sales teams that don't need marketing automation or operations management.

4. Freshsales — Best Free Plan After HubSpot (Free for 3 users)

Freshworks' CRM offering gives you a generous free tier for up to 3 users with contact management, deal tracking, and basic AI features. Paid plans start around ₱900/user/month.

Best for: Very small teams (1-3 people) who want a free CRM with room to grow.

5. Bitrix24 — Best Free Team Collaboration (Free for 5 users)

Bitrix24 combines CRM with project management, team chat, and even a website builder. The free plan supports 5 users with unlimited contacts. Paid plans start around ₱2,500/month for the team.

Best for: Teams that want an all-in-one workspace but don't need deep customization.

The Verdict: Which HubSpot Alternative Is Right for You?

If you need CRM plus operational workflows (approvals, project tracking, custom apps), Kintone gives you the most value per peso. If you're purely focused on email marketing and lead nurturing, Zoho or Freshsales might be enough. And if you're a sales-only team, Pipedrive's pipeline view is excellent.

The key question isn't 'which CRM is best?' — it's 'what does your business actually need beyond CRM?' If the answer is 'a lot,' Kintone is worth a serious look.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the cheapest HubSpot alternative in the Philippines?
Zoho CRM starts at ~₱800/user/month. Freshsales and Bitrix24 both offer free plans for small teams. Kintone starts at ₱1,000/user/month but includes operational workflows beyond CRM.
Can I migrate my data from HubSpot?
Yes. Most CRM platforms including Kintone support CSV import. For complex migrations with custom fields and deal history, Edamame Inc. provides hands-on migration support.
Is Kintone a CRM?
Kintone is a no-code platform — you can build a CRM, project management system, HR system, or any business app. Many Philippine companies use it as their CRM because they can customize it to match their exact sales process.
Does HubSpot work well in the Philippines?
HubSpot works globally, but Philippine businesses face USD pricing, no local support, and limited Tagalog/Filipino customization. Local alternatives offer peso pricing and Philippine-based support.
Which alternative has the best mobile app?
Pipedrive and Freshsales have excellent mobile apps. Kintone's mobile app supports full data access and form submission. Zoho's mobile experience is functional but can feel cluttered.
