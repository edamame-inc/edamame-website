Best Smartsheet Alternatives Philippines 2026: Project + Work Management for Less

Smartsheet too complex or too expensive? Compare the best alternatives for Philippine businesses — Kintone, Monday.com, Asana, Wrike, and ClickUp with peso pricing.

📅 2026-04-13 ⏱ 9 min read ✍️ Tom Arai, CEO — Edamame Inc.

Why Philippine Businesses Look Beyond Smartsheet

Smartsheet is a spreadsheet-powered project management tool popular with enterprises. But for Philippine businesses, the combination of USD pricing (~₱1,800+/user/month for Business plans), a spreadsheet-centric interface that alienates non-technical users, and limited workflow customization drives teams to explore alternatives.

1. Kintone — Best for Custom Business Applications (from ₱1,000/user/month)

Kintone replaces Smartsheet's spreadsheet approach with purpose-built business applications. Instead of managing projects in grid rows, you build dedicated apps with custom fields, views, and automated workflows. The result is cleaner, faster, and more intuitive for non-technical team members.

Best for: Companies that need work management integrated with CRM, HR, or operational workflows.

2. Monday.com — Best for Visual Boards (~₱500-1,500/user/month)

Monday.com is the most popular Smartsheet alternative globally. Visual boards replace spreadsheet rows with colorful, drag-and-drop interfaces. Strong automations and a growing app marketplace add flexibility.

Best for: Teams that prefer visual project management over spreadsheet-style views.

3. Asana — Best for Task-Centric Teams (Free for 15 users / ~₱600/user Pro)

Asana focuses on task management with multiple views (list, board, timeline, calendar). The free plan is generous at 15 users, and the interface is cleaner than Smartsheet's grid-heavy approach.

Best for: Teams focused on task assignments and deadlines rather than complex project tracking.

4. Wrike — Best for Marketing/Creative Teams (~₱550/user/month)

Wrike combines project management with proofing, time tracking, and resource management. Popular with marketing agencies and creative teams in the Philippines who need to manage campaigns and deliverables.

Best for: Marketing and creative agencies managing multiple client projects.

5. ClickUp — Best Value for Features (Free / ~₱350/user/month)

ClickUp packs more features into its free and low-cost plans than any other tool. Docs, whiteboards, goals, time tracking, and customizable views — all in one platform. The tradeoff is complexity.

Best for: Feature-hungry teams that don't mind a learning curve.

Making the Right Choice

If Smartsheet felt too rigid, Monday.com or Asana offer more flexibility at similar prices. If you need work management that extends into business operations (CRM, approvals, custom apps), Kintone provides the most complete solution. And if budget is your primary concern, ClickUp delivers remarkable value.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Smartsheet good for Philippine businesses?
Smartsheet works well for large enterprises with spreadsheet-savvy teams. For most Philippine SMEs, alternatives like Kintone or Monday.com offer better value and easier adoption.
What's cheaper than Smartsheet?
ClickUp (free or ~₱350/user), Asana (free for 15 users), and Kintone (₱1,000/user with full business app capabilities) all cost less than Smartsheet's Business plan.
Can Kintone replace Smartsheet?
Yes. Kintone handles project tracking, resource management, and reporting with custom apps. It adds workflow automation and CRM capabilities that Smartsheet lacks.
Is Monday.com better than Smartsheet?
For visual project management and ease of use, generally yes. Smartsheet is stronger for data-heavy, spreadsheet-style project tracking and enterprise reporting.
Does Smartsheet have a free plan?
Smartsheet offers a 30-day free trial but no permanent free plan. Asana, ClickUp, and Trello all have permanent free tiers.
