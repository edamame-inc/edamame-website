😤 Common Challenges in Healthcare Software

Philippine healthcare software companies face unique operational challenges that generic software can't solve. Here's what we hear from businesses every day:

Paper-Based Patient Records

Patient histories, prescriptions, and lab results stored in folders that get misfiled, damaged, or lost. Doctors waste time searching for charts instead of treating patients.

Appointment Chaos

Scheduling done via phone calls and notebooks. Double bookings, long wait times, and high no-show rates because there's no automated reminder system.

Manual Billing Nightmares

Computing PhilHealth deductions, HMO claims, and professional fees manually. Billing errors lead to delayed payments and patient complaints.

Supply & Medicine Tracking

Medical supplies and pharmacy inventory managed in spreadsheets. Expired medications, stockouts of critical supplies, and no visibility into consumption patterns.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Kintone compliant with Philippine data privacy laws?

Yes. Kintone is SOC 2 and ISO 27001 certified with enterprise-grade encryption. Combined with role-based access controls and audit trails, it meets the requirements of the Philippine Data Privacy Act (RA 10173) for handling sensitive personal information.

Can Kintone replace our existing hospital information system?

For most clinics and small-to-medium hospitals, yes. Kintone can handle patient records, scheduling, billing, inventory, and reporting. For large hospitals with complex departmental needs, Kintone often serves as a complementary system for specific workflows.

Does it support PhilHealth e-Claims?

Kintone can be configured to generate PhilHealth claim forms and track claim status. Integration with the PhilHealth e-Claims portal can be implemented through API or data export.

How do you handle patient data security?

Kintone provides encryption at rest and in transit, role-based access controls, IP restrictions, audit logging, and two-factor authentication. Data is stored in Cybozu's certified data centers.

Can doctors access records on mobile?

Yes. Kintone's mobile apps allow physicians to view patient records, receive lab notifications, and update clinical notes from their phones or tablets—securely and conveniently.
