⚠️ The Contact Tier Trap

HubSpot Marketing charges by contact tiers. 2,000 contacts included, then ₱1,300/month per additional 5,000. A Philippine business with 20,000 contacts pays an extra ~₱4,700/month just for contact storage — on top of the plan price.

🔍 Hidden Costs Most Philippine Businesses Miss

Mandatory Onboarding Fees

Professional plans require a one-time onboarding fee: ~₱170,000 for Marketing Pro, ~₱28,500 for Sales Pro. This is non-negotiable and non-refundable — even if you already know the platform.

Annual Lock-In

Professional and Enterprise plans require annual commitment. Cancel early? You still pay the full year. A 10-user Sales Pro commitment = ~₱615,000/year locked in.

Add-On Nickel-and-Diming

API calls beyond limits, additional reporting dashboards, extra users on shared inboxes — each costs extra. The "included" features often aren't enough for real operations.

Integration Costs

Connecting HubSpot to your Philippine accounting software, ERP, or custom tools typically requires a middleware service (₱2,000-15,000/month) or custom development.

✅ When HubSpot IS the Right Choice

💡 Honest Assessment

We're a Kintone partner, but we'll tell you straight: HubSpot genuinely wins in specific scenarios.

Choose HubSpot if:

  • Your business is primarily inbound marketing-driven (content, SEO, landing pages) — HubSpot's marketing automation is best-in-class
  • You need sophisticated email marketing with A/B testing, smart content, and detailed analytics
  • Your team is small (under 5) and the Free plan covers your needs — it's genuinely generous

Choose Kintone over HubSpot if:

  • You need CRM + operations (inventory, HR, approvals, project management) — HubSpot only does CRM and marketing
  • Your team is 10+ users and per-user costs matter
  • You need custom workflows specific to Philippine business processes

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is HubSpot free plan really free?

Yes, genuinely. You get contact management, deal tracking, email templates, and basic reporting at zero cost. The catch: HubSpot branding on forms/emails, limited automation, and you'll eventually want features that require paid plans.

Can I pay HubSpot monthly in Philippine pesos?

HubSpot charges in USD. Your Philippine bank or credit card handles the conversion, typically adding 1-3% foreign transaction fees on top of the listed price.

What's the cheapest way to use HubSpot in the Philippines?

Stay on the Free plan as long as possible. When you outgrow it, Starter at $15/user/month is reasonable. Avoid Professional unless inbound marketing is your core strategy — the jump from Starter to Professional is massive.

Is there a cheaper alternative to HubSpot for Philippine businesses?

For CRM + operations: Kintone (from ₱1,000/user/month) replaces HubSpot CRM plus adds custom apps. For marketing automation specifically: Brevo (starts ~₱4,000/month) handles email marketing at a fraction of HubSpot Marketing's cost.
