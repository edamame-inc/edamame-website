The Seed of Life

02 — The bean shape

Vesica Piscis

Where two circles of the seed overlap, they form an almond shape — the vesica piscis. This is the exact geometry of each edamame bean.

Two circles, radius R, centers R apart: Circle A: x² + y² = R² Circle B: (x − R)² + y² = R² Intersection: x = R/2, y = ±R√3/2 Vesica dimensions: Width = R Height = R√3 ≈ 1.7321 × R Aspect = √3 : 1 This is the bean shape.

The vesica piscis proportions are pure — governed entirely by √3, which emerges inevitably from the 60° hexagonal angle. It cannot be any other ratio. Two arcs, one shape, one number.
