01 — Origin

Seed of Life

Seven equal circles. 60-degree hexagonal symmetry. The grid from which all proportions grow.

7 circles, radius R, at 60 deg intervals sin 60 = sqrt(3)/2 This one value defines every coordinate.
02 — The bean

Vesica Piscis

Two circles side by side. The almond where they overlap is the bean — aspect ratio locked to sqrt(3).

Circles at (-R/2, 0) and (R/2, 0), both radius R Intersection: x=0, y=+/- R*sqrt(3)/2 Bean width = R Bean height = R * sqrt(3) Ratio = sqrt(3) : 1 = 1.732 : 1
03 — Golden center

The ratio phi

Center bean from circles of radius phi*R. Exactly 1.618x the sides in every dimension. Same aspect. Pure scaling.

Side beans: radius R -> W=R, H=R*sqrt(3) Center bean: radius phi*R -> W=phi*R, H=phi*R*sqrt(3) Center / Side = phi = 1.618034 Area ratio = phi^2 = 2.618
